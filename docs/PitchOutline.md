# CoCraft 2-Minute Hackathon Pitch Outline

## Goal
Make CoCraft feel like the next step after passive stories and generic chatbots: a live storytelling partner that helps people create emotionally coherent stories in real time.

## Core Message
CoCraft turns storytelling from something you consume into something you shape.

## 2-Minute Structure

### 0:00-0:15 - Hook
Open with a relatable contrast:

"Most AI story tools write for you. CoCraft writes with you."

Then give one vivid setup:

"Imagine starting with only a character, a mood, and a choice. Two minutes later, you are inside a story that remembers what you did and adapts to what you care about."

### 0:15-0:35 - Problem
Frame the gap:

- Passive stories are engaging but fixed.
- Role-playing and creative writing are flexible but high effort.
- AI chat can generate text, but it often feels aimless, forgetful, or emotionally flat.

The problem statement:

"People do not just want infinite content. They want stories that respond, remain coherent, and feel like they are becoming theirs."

### 0:35-0:55 - Product
Introduce CoCraft plainly:

"CoCraft is a storytelling harness for real-time co-creation. The user provides intent, emotional direction, and choices. The system tracks narrative state, remembers what happened, and generates meaningful next possibilities."

Emphasize the harness, not only the model:

"The magic is not just text generation. It is the structure around the model: state, memory, constraints, emotional goals, and branching futures."

### 0:55-1:25 - Demo Arc
Show one compact scenario:

1. User chooses an audience, tone, and story seed.
2. CoCraft begins the scene and offers meaningful next directions.
3. User makes an unexpected choice.
4. CoCraft adapts while preserving continuity, emotional tone, and narrative momentum.

Narration during demo:

"Notice that the system is not forcing a predefined ending. It is maintaining the story world while opening possible futures."

### 1:25-1:40 - Why It Wins
Connect to hackathon judging:

- Clear prototype: interactive, public-facing, easy to try.
- Strong use of AI: LLM plus memory, state, and adaptation.
- Differentiated experience: co-creation instead of content generation.
- Measurable path: engagement, coherence, satisfaction, and reduced artificiality.

### 1:40-1:55 - Impact
Broaden the use cases:

"Today this is for storytellers, role-players, and creative experimenters. Next, it can support educators, facilitators, game designers, and anyone who wants to explore ideas through narrative."

### 1:55-2:00 - Close
End with the product principle:

"CoCraft does not just tell a story. It helps people create one in real time."

## Suggested Speaker Notes

- Keep the language human and concrete. Avoid over-explaining architecture.
- Spend more time on the experience than the feature list.
- Use one memorable example rather than several abstract use cases.
- Make the demo feel like proof of emotional intelligence, not just branching logic.
- Repeat the core contrast: "writes with you, not for you."

## Optional One-Sentence Taglines

- "CoCraft is a real-time storytelling partner for stories that remember, adapt, and become yours."
- "CoCraft turns AI storytelling into live co-creation."
- "From passive narrative to participatory imagination."
